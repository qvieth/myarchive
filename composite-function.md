# 10 what are composite functions? evaluating composition of functions and examples
- https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8-HZighI0oQ
``` 
         COMPOSITE FUNCTION
          +------+     +------+
  x +---> + g(x) +---->+ f(x) +---> f(g(x))
          +------+     +------+
```
- also called :
    - chained functions
    - nested functions
* go from inner most and work your way out
